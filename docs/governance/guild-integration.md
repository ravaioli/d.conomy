# Guild Integration

d.conomy components are managed by **Guilds** (verified collectives) through CivicNotes.

## Guild Roles

- **LoT Steward** – Manages inventory, sets borrowing policies.
- **Mutual Credit Circle Administrator** – Enforces circle rules, processes new members.
- **CLT Board Member** – Represents the Guild on the CLT board.
- **Φ‑Currency Policy Advocate** – Proposes changes to minting/demurrage via CivicNotes.

## Guild Decision Making

- All major decisions (e.g., acquiring a new tool, changing demurrage rate) are made via **CivicNotes proposals**.
- Proposals are visible to all members and subject to comment periods.
- Guilds may use their own internal governance (consensus, majority vote) to decide how to vote on proposals.

## Accountability

- Guilds must report d.conomy activities in their **annual transparency report** (published on CivicNotes).
- Failure to report or misuse of resources can lead to suspension of Guild privileges (see dispute resolution).
