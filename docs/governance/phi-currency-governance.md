# Φ‑Currency Governance

The Φ‑currency system is governed by the **Guild Council** (one representative per Guild) and subject to community oversight via CivicNotes.

## Policy Areas

- **Minting Parameters** – Exchange rates for PoR events, maximum supply.
- **Demurrage Rate** – Adjusted periodically based on circulation velocity.
- **Redistribution** – How collected demurrage is used (e.g., community grants, infrastructure).

## Proposal Process

1. Any Guild or group of 10 members can submit a Φ‑currency policy proposal via CivicNotes.
2. The proposal enters a 14‑day comment period.
3. The Guild Council votes; a supermajority (e.g., 60%) is required for adoption.
4. Approved changes are implemented by the technical team.

## Dispute Resolution

- Disputes about individual transactions or PoR events are handled by the **Moderation Council** (see CivicNotes governance).
- Disputes about policy are resolved through the above proposal process.
